Hello World!

This is my first webpage, and this is a paragraph about me. Not sure just how detailed we need to be, but I'll start with my college career. I spent the first year or two stuck between wanting to go into pharmacy or UX/UI design. Ultimately, as we may know, I chose design. I realized I spent a lot of my middle school and high school extra curriculars taking design or animation classes and took that as a sign. I've never been much of a academiclly science driven person so I worried pharmacy and med. school would be really difficult for me. I picked design over animation because I already knew what the animation industry would look like for me and I KNEW I didn't want that in my future. So I spent the first two years of my college life at Collin College finishing my core classes before transfering here to UTD to start my journey in Media Arts and Design.

This is a list of my inspirational websites~

  • Pokemon Explorer

    I like this site because it shows us all statistical information about pokemon from any generation. The only feature I wish it had was what pokemon type its weak and strong against.

  • Minimal Wim

    This was a really cool and inspiring website that animated how these designers designed and developed a simple font. It was cool in the sense of getting to see the font form in front of our eyes. This was inspiring in the way that seeing the thought process of how people created letters and numbers from a three by three grid makes me realize that anything is possible. The only negative thing for me is that the letters and numbers became hard to read and differenciate.

  • r/assholedesign

    A classic website: Reddit. While I don't use it everyday, there will be times where I need a question answered and Reddit has just what I need. Opinionated people speak their minds on here and, in terms of design, this can be a very helpful tool to know what designs people are enjoying or hating.
